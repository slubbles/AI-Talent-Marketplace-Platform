# **Scope of Work**

## **AI Talent Marketplace Platform**

**Web \+ Mobile Talent Supply & Demand Management System**

---

# **1\. Project Overview**

The project aims to develop an **AI-powered Talent Marketplace Platform** that connects enterprise talent demand with a global supply of consultants and professionals.

Unlike traditional Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), the platform provides a **holistic demand-supply intelligence system**, enabling organizations to forecast talent needs, match talent intelligently, and manage workforce mobility and onboarding.

The platform will combine:

* Talent marketplace functionality  
* AI-based talent matching  
* Demand forecasting  
* Workforce analytics  
* Mobility and onboarding support services

The solution will operate as:

* **Mobile App → for Talent / Consultants**  
* **Web Application → for Recruiters, Hiring Managers, and Portfolio Companies**  
* **Admin & Governance Platform → for platform operators**

The system will create a **single view of demand and supply**, enabling portfolio organizations to optimize talent acquisition, reduce hiring costs, and access global expertise.
